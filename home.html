SIGNATURE DISH

ABOUT US


GRAEXDINO was founded in 2002 by Joel Ko who spotted a opportuntity to broaden his market by bringing this restaurant to Singapore.It was originated from Italy.We hope to deliver superior quality ,with tastiness of every bite you consume,bringing satisfaction to your fingertips.  



Inspired by Wolfgang Puck, GRAEXDINO is based on the urban ethos of being brave enough to change the game. We're shaking up the dining scene with an ambience that defies tradition, modern AND even fusion dishes peppered with our own touch, and a amazing Singapore mixed fusion cultural food being prepped in with delicacies that grabs your attention.
